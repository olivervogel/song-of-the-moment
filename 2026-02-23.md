title: "Here"
year: 2026
---

There are bold artistic performances, maximalist productions and emotional
pyrotechnics and then there is "Here" by Aoife Wolf. 

Originally written and performed by Pavement in 1992, the song already has this
quality of sounding accidental on purpose. Wolf's cover however takes this to
the next level and gently removes even the suggestion of an effort.

The result has a distinctly demo-like feel. Just this low-key guitar strumming
with all the urgency of a houseplant photosynthesizing in winter and Wolf's
fragile voice. One might ask, did anyone here even feel like it? 

And yet it works. The song creates a unique dark atmosphere, proving
that sometimes the boldest artistic statement is a well-executed shrug.
